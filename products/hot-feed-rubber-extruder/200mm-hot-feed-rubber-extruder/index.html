What is a Hot Feed Rubber Extruder?

A hot feed rubber extruder is a specialized machine designed to process pre-heated rubber compounds from mixing mills or kneaders. Unlike cold feed extruders that accept room-temperature rubber, hot feed extruders work with material that has already been plasticized and warmed, allowing for higher output rates and better flow characteristics for certain applications.

The rotating screw pushes the preheated rubber through a custom-designed die, creating consistent profiles, sheets, tubes, or other shapes. Our hot feed extruders range from 40mm to 200mm screw diameters, with L/D ratios optimized for various rubber compounds and production requirements.

We also manufacture cold feed rubber extruders and rubber mixing mills for complete rubber processing solutions. Contact our engineering team for custom configurations.

Key Features:

  • Processes pre-heated rubber compounds for higher output
  • Interchangeable dies for multiple profiles
  • Hardened screw and barrel for wear resistance
  • Precise temperature control with oil/water circulation
  • Feed roll system for automatic strip feeding
