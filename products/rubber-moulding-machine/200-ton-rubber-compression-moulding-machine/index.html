What is a Rubber Moulding Machine?

A rubber moulding machine is an industrial press used to shape and cure rubber compounds into finished products through compression, transfer, or injection molding processes. These machines apply precise heat and pressure to transform raw rubber into durable components with complex geometries.

Our rubber moulding machines range from 50 tons to 400 tons capacity, featuring pillar type and frame type constructions. The electrically heated platens ensure uniform temperature distribution for consistent curing, while PLC-based controls provide precise cycle management.

We also manufacture rubber dispersion kneaders and rubber mixing mills for complete rubber processing solutions. Contact our engineering team for custom configurations.

Key Features:

  • Compression Moulding from 50 to 400 Tons capacity
  • Pillar type and H-Type frame constructions
  • Electrical platen heating for uniform curing
  • PLC-based automatic cycle control
  • Customizable platen sizes up to 1000 x 1000 mm

Applications of Rubber Moulding Machines

Automotive Components

Engine mounts, bushings, seals, gaskets, and vibration dampers

Industrial Products

O-rings, diaphragms, rollers, and custom molded parts

Consumer Goods

Footwear soles, grips, handles, and rubber mats
