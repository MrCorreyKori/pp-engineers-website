What is a Rubber Dispersion Kneader?

A rubber dispersion kneader is a high-intensity internal mixer designed for processing rubber compounds, plastics, and other polymeric materials. It consists of a mixing chamber with two counter-rotating rotors that provide high shear mixing through advanced intermeshing or tangential rotor designs.

The hydraulic tilting mechanism allows for easy discharge of mixed compounds, while the heavy-duty gearbox ensures consistent power transmission even under full load conditions. Modern dispersion kneaders feature PLC automation for precise temperature control and mixing cycle management.

We also manufacture rubber mixing mills and extrusion systems for complete rubber processing solutions. Contact our engineering team for custom configurations.

Key Working Principles:

  • High shear mixing between rotors and chamber wall
  • Intensive material circulation and dispersion
  • Precise temperature control through jacketed chamber
  • Hydraulic tilting for rapid material discharge
