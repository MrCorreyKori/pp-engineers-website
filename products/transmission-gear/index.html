What are Power Transmission Gears?

Power transmission gears are mechanical components used to transmit motion and torque between machine parts. They are essential in various industrial applications where precise speed control, torque multiplication, and directional changes are required. Our precision-engineered gears ensure efficient power transmission with minimal noise and vibration.

We manufacture a comprehensive range of industrial gears including spur gears for parallel shaft applications, helical gears for smoother and quieter operation, bevel gears for angular transmissions, and worm gears for high reduction ratios. Each gear is manufactured using high-grade materials with accurate tooth profiles for optimal performance.

We also manufacture rubber mixing mills and rubber extruders that utilize these precision gears. Contact our engineering team for custom gear manufacturing requirements.

Gear Types Available:

  • Spur Gears: Straight teeth, parallel shafts
  • Helical Gears: Angled teeth, smoother operation
  • Bevel Gears: Intersecting shafts, angular transmission
  • Worm Gears: High reduction, right-angle drives
  • Custom Gears: As per customer specifications
