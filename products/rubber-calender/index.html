What is a Rubber Calender Machine?

A rubber calender machine is a precision industrial equipment used to process rubber compounds into sheets, films, or coatings of uniform thickness. It consists of multiple heated rolls that squeeze and compress the rubber material to achieve the desired thickness and surface finish.

Our three-roll calenders feature chilled cast iron rolls with Shore hardness 70-75, ensuring excellent wear resistance and consistent product quality. The motorized nip adjustment provides precision of �0.01 mm, while the automatic temperature control with PID controllers maintains optimal processing conditions between 150°C to 200°C.
