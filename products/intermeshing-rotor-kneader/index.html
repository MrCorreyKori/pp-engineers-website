What is an Intermeshing Rotor Kneader?

An intermeshing rotor kneader is a high-performance internal mixer designed for efficient and uniform processing of rubber compounds. Unlike tangential rotors, intermeshing rotors interlock, creating high shear forces ideal for high-viscosity materials, carbon-black compounds, and heavy-duty industrial rubber applications.
