About P & P Engineers & Contractor

Established in 2011, P & P Engineers & Contractor is a manufacturer of Rubber Dispersion Kneader Machines, Mixing Mills, Moulding Machines, Extruders and Intermix Rotors based in Ambarnath MIDC, Maharashtra.

Backed by over 30 years of rubber industry experience, we deliver heavy-duty industrial machinery designed for durability, performance and precision.
